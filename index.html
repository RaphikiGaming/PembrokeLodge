Pembroke Lodge suggests for your wedding his unique grade II listed Georgian Mansion situated at the highest point of the largest Royal Park within London. A perfect venue for traditional weddings

Offering two options for your wedding ceremony, reception and evening wedding party. The first one is The Belvedere and the second one is The Russel suite.

The Belvedere allows to gather 140 person for a wedding ceremony and the reception. And up to 200 for evening wedding party. This room is situated on the ground floor and have a private outdoor terrace with spectacular sweeping views over the Thames Valley.

The Russel suite allow to gather 70 person for a wedding ceremony and the reception. And up to 100 for evening wedding party. This room is situated on the first floor, will make you enjoy views out of the West. The South Lawn garden directly below the Russel Suite is available for outdoor ceremonies.
